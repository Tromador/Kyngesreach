Profile: Sergeant Bridget Mallory (Marine Detachment Second-in-Command)

---

Basic Information:
- Name: Bridget Mallory
- Age: 38
- Race: Human
- Gender: Female
- Rank: Sergeant (Marines)
- Role: Second-in-command of the marine detachment; de facto leader of the marines in the field.

---

Appearance:
- Weathered face with scars that tell stories she never shares.
- Close-cropped hair for practicality, streaked with early grays.
- Eyes like cold steel—calculating and unflinching.
- Wears a battered uniform that’s seen countless engagements.
- Physically imposing with a muscular build and intimidating presence.

---

Personality:
- Hard, ruthless, and unapologetically blunt.
- Holds her marines to high standards and tolerates no excuses.
- Views the marine captain with contempt, seeing him as a liability.
- Protects her marines fiercely but expects them to earn that protection.
- Possesses a dark sense of humor, often at someone else’s expense.

---

Motivations:
- Believes in strength, discipline, and earning one’s place.
- Sees this voyage as another battlefield where weakness isn’t tolerated.
- Driven to keep her squad alive—despite her disdain for the captain’s leadership.

---

Strengths:
- Skilled tactician in small-unit combat.
- Commands respect through sheer presence and battlefield experience.
- Deadly with both musket and cutlass; competent with pistols.
- Exceptional at motivating troops through fear and respect.

---

Weaknesses:
- Overly aggressive; quick to resort to violence.
- Holds grudges that can cloud her judgment.
- Has little patience for diplomacy or formalities.
- Tends to undermine the captain in front of the men.

---

Relationships:
- **Marine Captain:** Despises his weakness; follows orders only when unavoidable.
- **Marines:** Respected and feared; they follow her without question.
- **Officers:** Keeps interactions minimal, preferring to focus on her squad.
- **Players:** Could clash with them if she perceives them as coddled or incompetent.

---

Quotes:
- "If you can't keep up, I’ll leave you behind. Simple."
- "The captain says jump? I say, don’t bother unless I tell you to."
- "Pain builds character. Let’s see how much character you’ve got left."

---

Potential Plot Hooks & Interactions:
- Might recruit players for “unofficial” missions outside the captain’s orders.
- Could challenge a player’s authority if tensions escalate.
- May reveal hidden depths of loyalty in dire circumstances.
- Her disdain for the captain could lead to dangerous friction during crises.

---

Additional Information & Notes:
- Known for settling disputes with fists rather than words.
- Has a private stash of weapons she maintains better than ship-issued arms.
- Keeps a tally of confirmed kills etched into her musket’s stock.
- Would sacrifice the captain without hesitation if it meant saving her marines.

Relationships:
- **Captain Elias Godfrey:** Respects his no-nonsense leadership; follows his orders without complaint.
- **First Lieutenant Isabella Hargrave:** Thinks she’s soft but appreciates her brains when planning assaults.
- **Second Lieutenant Alaric Drake:** Sees a kindred warrior spirit; partners well in combat.
- **Boatswain Margaret "Iron Meg" Kincaid:** Rarely speaks, yet they understand each other perfectly.
- **Marine Captain Edward Pembroke:** Loathes his incompetence; keeps him alive only out of duty.
- **Surgeon Archibald Catesby:** Wary of him; doesn’t trust drunks but respects his skills.
- **Nyxie (Ship’s Cat):** Ignores her unless she interferes with weapons storage.
- **Crew:** Tolerates them but doesn’t coddle; expects backbone.
- **Marines:** Her responsibility; will die for them if it comes to it.
- **Players:** Judges them by their battlefield mettle.
