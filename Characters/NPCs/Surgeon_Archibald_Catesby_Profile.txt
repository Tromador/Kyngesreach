Profile: Surgeon Archibald Catesby

---

Basic Information:
- Name: Archibald Catesby
- Age: 42
- Race: Human
- Gender: Male
- Rank: Surgeon
- Role: Ship’s medical officer, responsible for treating injuries and illnesses.

---

Appearance:
- Lean with sharp features and graying hair.
- Often wears a stained apron over his uniform.
- Smells faintly of alcohol and medicinal herbs.

---

Personality:
- Wry and sarcastic, especially when dealing with injuries he deems avoidable.
- Prone to grumbling but performs under pressure.
- Battles his own demons, often choosing liquor over rest.

---

Motivations:
- To fulfill his duty while battling personal vices.
- Hopes to redeem his reputation through competence on this expedition.

---

Strengths:
- Highly skilled in surgery and herbal remedies.
- Quick-thinking in medical emergencies.
- Deeply knowledgeable, despite his flaws.

---

Weaknesses:
- Alcoholism affects his consistency.
- Stubborn, resistant to unsolicited advice.
- His past mistakes weigh heavily on him.

---


Quotes:
- "I patch you up; you try not to die again. Deal?"
- "It’s not the cleanest place, but you’ll live. Probably."
- "Pain builds character—or so I hear."

---

Potential Plot Hooks & Interactions:
- May struggle with sobriety at critical times.
- His knowledge of old medical texts might uncover hidden cures or curses.
- Could clash with players over risky medical procedures.

---

Additional Information & Notes:
- Carries a personal flask, seldom empty.
- Keeps surgical tools meticulously clean despite his personal mess.
- Occasionally hums old sea shanties while working.


Relationships:
- **Captain Elias Godfrey:** Quietly respects his leadership; appreciates that he doesn’t micromanage his work.
- **First Lieutenant Isabella Hargrave:** Finds her inquisitiveness both helpful and annoying.
- **Second Lieutenant Alaric Drake:** Bets on how often he’ll treat him; finds his recklessness amusing.
- **Boatswain Margaret "Iron Meg" Kincaid:** Respects her ability to keep the crew in line.
- **Marine Captain Edward Pembroke:** Drinks with him; considers him more tolerable when quiet.
- **Marine Sergeant Bridget Mallory:** Wary of her intensity; grateful she keeps the marines alive.
- **Nyxie (Ship’s Cat):** Shoos her away from medical supplies; secretly enjoys her company.
- **Crew:** Regards them as accident-prone; treats them with gruff efficiency.
- **Marines:** Frequent patients; scolds them while patching them up.
- **Players:** Helps them as long as they don’t waste his time.
